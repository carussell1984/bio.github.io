Latitude vs. Humidity

Cities closest to the equator are more likely to have high humidity compared to cities further away from the equator.

Approximatley 75 % of cities most closest to the equator (between -10 and +10 degrees), have humidity percentages between 75% to 100%, and have an average humidity of 82.38% (± 1 std = 12.96 %).

The further south and the further north one moves from the equator, the more variation and the range in humidity can be observed amongst cities. The northern hemisphere experiences the widest range humidity from 0 – 100 %, between 15 to 45 degrees north of the equator.
