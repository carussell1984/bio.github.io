Latitude vs. Windspeed

No correlation can be concluded with respect to Windspeed, as one approaches the equator.

The average windspeed across all latitudes in this dataset (between -54.81 degrees and 78.22 degrees) is 7.63 MPH, and 75% of the cities across all latitudes experience windspeeds ranging from 0.34 MPH to 10.29 MPH. Less than one percent of cities have windspeeds higher than 25 MPH. The max windspeed in the dataset was 34.45 MPH (City: Karasjok, Country: Norway, Latitude: 69.47).
